Khata Book

a Mobile Application built with Flutter

A digital bookkeeping application designed to help small businesses and shop owners manage customer credit, debit, and transactions efficiently.

  • Track customer credit and debit transactions in real time
  • Maintain complete transaction history with clear balances
  • Payment reminders to improve collection and cash flow
  • E-commerce integration for managing online sales and customers
  • Inventory tracking with profit and loss visibility
  • MVC-based architecture for scalability and maintainability
  • Secure backend using Firebase (auth, database, storage)
