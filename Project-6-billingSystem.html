Billing management system

a Desktop-based Application with javax

My billing management system is a robust solution built with Java, AWT, and Javax that provides a plethora of features to streamline your billing process. With this system, you can easily add, update, or delete customers, as well as add and update items to your inventory. Generating bills is a breeze with this system, as you can simply select the items purchased by the customer, and the system will automatically calculate the total amount. Additionally, you can view and manage past bills, ensuring that all of your billing data is organized and easily accessible. Overall, this billing management system is a powerful tool that will help you save time, reduce errors, and improve the overall efficiency of your billing process.
